Our Story

A Secret Garden
Born from Love

Nestled deep within the lush tea gardens of Sukalarang, Suka Village was born from a simple dream — to create a sanctuary where modern comfort meets untouched nature. What once was a quiet family estate has blossomed into a beloved retreat for those seeking peace away from the city's noise.

Every cabin, every corner of our grounds, has been shaped with intention — to honour the land and give you space to reconnect with what truly matters.

"The forest does not hurry, yet everything is accomplished."
— The Philosophy of Suka Village
